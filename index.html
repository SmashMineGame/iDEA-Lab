Marketing Skills

Through iDEA Lab, students will learn marketing skills such as, how to find the correct target audience, how to make ideas appeal to a target audience, and how to communicate to the target audience in a way that convinces them to do what you want.

Presentation Skills

Through iDEA Lab, students will learn presentation skills. These skills include, creating a presentation that keeps the audience's attention, articulating a script to show knowledge of a topic or idea, and using colors or other types of visuals to make a presentation fit a theme and look good.

Designer Skills

Through iDEA Lab, students will learn design skills. Some of these skills are, using color theory to create a webpage or presentation that shows emotion or cause, creating things which has a structure that is appealing to the target audience, and other design principles which will make something which does not look crowded but also does not look empty.
