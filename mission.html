What We Are Here For

Students have a lot of great ideas but don’t have a chance to show them all. When students have a good idea that they are willing to develope themselves, schools discourage them because they don't have a good reason for change. Even though there is no reason to change, schools should still allow change to happen if students are willing to to spend time and effort developing their ideas. The students will gain some skills and the school will get improved, which helps both sides of the problem.

Also, schools aren’t teaching non-academic based skills, which are the important ones which they will use in the future. Some students get very surprised when they get to college and they have to use non-academic based skills, such as time-managements, during their everyday lives.
